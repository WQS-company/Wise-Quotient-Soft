African Classes Initiative

Student Rules & Regulations

General Rules
  • Students must attend classes regularly and be punctual.
  • Respect for instructors and fellow students is mandatory.
  • Students should maintain a clean and safe learning environment.
  • Use of abusive language or fighting is strictly prohibited.
  • Students must not damage any class property.
Enrollment and Participation
  • All personal information submitted must be correct and verifiable.
  • Students must select at least one class day and preferred time.
  • Enrollment is valid only after confirmation by African Classes.
  • Students should participate actively and complete assigned tasks.
Communication and Conduct
  • Students must join the official learning channel (WhatsApp, YouTube, etc.).
  • Students should follow instructions given by instructors in the learning group.
  • Any form of cheating or impersonation will lead to removal.
Safety and Responsibility
  • Students are responsible for their own devices and belongings.
  • African Classes is not liable for loss or damage of personal items.
  • Students must not share harmful or illegal content in class groups.
Certification
  • Students will only receive certificates after completing the program.
  • Consistent attendance and participation are required for certification.

By enrolling, you agree to abide by all the above rules and regulations.

Dokokin Gabaɗaya
  • Dalibai dole ne su halarci darasi akai-akai kuma su kasance masu zuwa akan lokaci.
  • Girmama masu koyarwa da sauran dalibai wajibi ne.
  • Dalibai su kiyaye tsafta da tsaro a wajen karatu.
  • Amfani da kalaman cin zarafi ko faɗa an haramta.
  • Dalibai ba su da izinin lalata kayan aji.
Shiga da Halartar Darasi
  • Dukkan bayanan da aka cika dole ne su kasance daidai.
  • Dalibi dole ne ya zaɓi ranakun da zai halarci darasi da lokacin da ya fi so.
  • Shigar dalibi zai tabbata ne bayan African Classes ta tabbatar.
  • Dalibai su yi aiki tare kuma su kammala ayyukan da aka ba su.
Sadarwa da Ɗabi’a
  • Dalibi dole ne ya shiga tashar karatu kamar WhatsApp ko YouTube.
  • Dalibai su bi duk umarnin da aka bayar daga masu koyarwa a cikin rukuni.
  • Duk wani nau’in magudi ko amfani da sunan wani zai sa a kore dalibi.
Tsaro da Alhaki
  • Dalibi na da alhakin kayansa da na’urorinsa.
  • African Classes ba ta da alhakin asarar kayan dalibi.
  • Ba a yarda a raba abu mai cutarwa ko haram a rukuni ba.
Takardar Shaida
  • Dalibi zai samu takardar shaida ne bayan ya kammala karatun.
  • Dole ne ya halarci darasi akai-akai domin samun takardar.

Ta hanyar yin rajista, ka amince da dukkan dokokin da ke sama.
